Frequently Asked Questions about Pixera Screenshot Styling

    • Does Pixera work with all screenshot tools?

      Yes! Pixera detects screenshots from macOS or third-party tools instantly and opens the editor automatically.

    • Is my data processed online?

      No. Pixera is a completely offline macOS app. All image processing happens on your device, ensuring full privacy.

    • Can I create custom gradient styles?

      Absolutely. You can save and reuse your favorite gradient sets, adjust directions, and customize them freely.

    • How does Pixera detect sensitive data?

      Pixera uses on-device heuristics to detect patterns like emails, API keys, and card numbers—and redacts them automatically.

    • Does the free version include all features?

      The free version offers the core editor. Pixera Premium unlocks batch exports, watermark removal, redaction, and advanced styling.

    • Can I export in high resolution?

      Yes! All exports are pixel-perfect and crisp, regardless of your screen resolution.

    • Is there a trial for Premium?

      There is no trial, but Pixera offers a free version with core editing tools. Upgrade to Premium for advanced styling and privacy features.

    • Can I style multiple screenshots at once?

      With Pixera Premium, you can batch style and export multiple screenshots to save time.
